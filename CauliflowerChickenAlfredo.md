# Cauliflower Chicken Alfredo

## Ingredients

- 5 oz bacon, diced
- 2 lbs boneless chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 6 cups baby spinach
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
- 1 &frac14; lbs cauliflower, cut into small florets
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Fry the bacon until crispy. Drain and set aside in a bowl or on a plate.
2. Cut the chicken into &frac12; - 1 inch pieces. Add butter to the same skillet used for the bacon, keeping the bacon fat. Add chicken and garlic. Saut&eacute; until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
3. Set chicken aside in a separate bowl. Saut&eacute; the spinach until it starts to wilt, but no more. Set aside.
4. Add heavy cream to pan and bring it to a slight boil, scraping the fond from the bottom of the pan.
5. Add parmesan cheese, bacon, chicken, and spinach to the pan. Salt and pepper to taste.
6. Lower the heat to medium low and let simmer while preparing the cauliflower.
7. Parboil the cauliflower in lightly salted water for about 5 minutes. Drain thoroughly.
8. Stir cauliflower into the sauce or combine into a bigger serving bowl.
