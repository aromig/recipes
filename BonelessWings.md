# Boneless Chicken Wings

## Ingredients

- 10-12 oz chicken breast or thigh
- 1 tbsp neutral oil
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- &frac12; tbsp paprika
- salt & pepper to taste
- &frac14; cup cornstarch
- wing sauce (e.g. buffalo, teriyaki, garlic parmesan, etc)

## Directions

1. Cut meat into ~1.5 inch pieces and pat dry with a paper towel.
2. Combine seasonings and cornstarch together in a large bowl.
3. Add chicken pieces to another bowl and toss with the oil to coat.
4. Add chicken to seasoning bowl and toss until evenly coated.
5. Place the seasoned chicken pieces in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
6. Cook on air fry setting at 400&deg; for 12-15 minutes. Flip or shake chicken halfway through cooking. Be sure the chicken is done and the internal temperature is at least 165&deg;F.
7. Transfer chicken to a large bowl and toss with your favorite wing sauce.
8. Enjoy with or without a dipping sauce!
