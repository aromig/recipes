# Ground Turkey Stroganoff

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 can (10.5oz) cream of mushroom soup
- &frac12; tsp salt
- &frac12; tsp pepper
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp honey dijon mustard
- &frac12; cup heavy cream
- &frac12; cup cut carrots
- 8 oz extra-wide egg noodles, cooked per package directions

## Directions

1. Brown the ground turkey in medium or large pan until done.
2. Add carrots, garlic, salt, and pepper to the meat. Let cook on medium high for a couple of minutes.
3. Mix in the chicken broth, mushroom soup, Worcestershire sauce, and honey mustard.
4. Let simmer on medium high for about 10 minutes. You can cook the egg noodles during this time if preferred.
5. Mix in heavy cream and egg noodles. Lower heat to medium and let simmer for about 4 minutes.
